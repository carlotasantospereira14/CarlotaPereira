NICE TO MEET YOU,

I've always been a very creative person with lots of interests in all hobbies that involve using my hands to create something. Whether it's through drawing, painting, or crafting, I find joy in turning my ideas into real creations.

I love drawing and painting, especially with watercolors, as the fluidity and unpredictability of the medium always lead to unique results. Recently, I've ventured into sewing my own clothes, which has opened up a new creative outlet for me. Designing, selecting fabrics, and putting everything together has become a fulfilling way to express myself. Alongside that, I've also tried bead embroidery, adding unique, detailed touches to my pieces.

Bright colors, bold patterns, and girly aesthetics are my go-to inspirations. I love mixing unexpected elements in my projects to create something original and full of personality. Every new project I take on is an opportunity to explore new ideas, challenge myself, and bring a little more creativity into my world.

Balancing my studies with my passion for hands-on projects allows me to explore new techniques and ideas while developing my skills. Every class I take and every assignment I complete contributes to my artistic journey, helping me grow both personally and professionally as I prepare for my future in the creative field.
